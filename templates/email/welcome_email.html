Terms and Conditions

  1. The course fee must be paid in full at least 7days prior to the commencement of the course.
  2. Payment has to be made by online transfer / credit / debit card / Cheque / DD.
  3. Remaining Payment has to be made on or before 30 days from the course start date. (only for who have paid the part payment)
  4. Fee once paid is valid for a period of six months before which the course has to be completed or else the admission stands cancelled and the fee will not be refunded.
  5. Minimum number of students required to start a online batch is 6 and for offline batch is 10.
  6. Fee once paid will not be refunded. inlingua Chennai refunds the fee collected only if inlingua drops
  7. the batch due to unavoidable situations. If the student wants to withdraw his/her admission prior to
  8. the commencement of the course, a processing fee of Rs 2500/- would be deducted. Once the class
  9. commences, there will be no refund provided.
  10. inlingua reserves the right to change the trainer of any ongoing batch at any point of time for a valid reason.
  11. A minimum attendance of 80% is required to be eligible to take up the inlingua examination at the
  12. end of the course. If a student has less than 80% attendance and above 50% attendance, he/she
  13. will be provided participation certificates only.
  14. All back up/missed classes will be charged at the rate of Rs.800/- per hour. Such sessions will be planned and executed solely based on the schedule provided by inlingua only.
  15. Transfer from one batch to another batch would be accepted only before the commencement of the
  16. course. The same would be decided based on the availability of seats in the desired batch.
  17. Under no circumstances will the postponement of final exam date would be entertained. However, students who are unable to take the examination due to valid reasons, will be allotted an alternate day on paying an additional fee of Rs.2500/- towards examination charges.
  18. Certificates will be processed in 10 working days from the date of completion of the examination from the respective centers.
